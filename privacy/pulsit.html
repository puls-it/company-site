Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PULS IT ("we", "us", or "our") handles personal data when you visit our website or use our mobile applications.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable EU laws.

2. Data We Collect

Our website is fully static and does not collect or process any personal information. We do not use cookies, forms, analytics, or tracking tools on the website.

Some of our iOS applications may, in the future, collect limited anonymous usage data (e.g., event statistics via Google Analytics for Firebase) for performance and improvement purposes. This data does not identify users personally.

3. How We Use Data

Any data collected within our mobile apps is used solely to improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience. We never sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for advertising or tracking purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain anonymous analytics data only for as long as necessary to analyze performance trends and app usage. No personally identifiable information is stored on our servers.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data (if any). You can contact us anytime at contact@pulsit.dev.

6. Third-Party Services

Some of our mobile apps may use third-party APIs or SDKs such as Google Analytics for Firebase or YouTube Data API. These services process limited data in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Information

Email: contact@pulsit.dev

For product-specific policies, please refer to the individual Privacy Policies available on each product page.
