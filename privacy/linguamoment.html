Effective Date: November 2025

LinguaMoment connects language learners for live video conversations and AI‑assisted tutoring.

1. Data We Access

  • Camera and microphone (only during active calls)
  • Username or profile information (stored locally)
  • AI chat messages (processed securely, not stored permanently)

2. How We Use Data

We use your data only to enable live communication, improve call quality, and provide language feedback features. No personal data is shared with third parties.

3. Security

All connections are encrypted using industry‑standard protocols. We never record calls or conversations without consent.

4. Contact

For questions, contact support@pulsit.dev.
