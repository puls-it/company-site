TubeTransfer Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

TubeTransfer uses the official YouTube Data API to transfer users’ playlists, subscriptions, and liked videos between accounts. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Access

  • Limited YouTube account access via OAuth 2.0
  • No external servers or data storage

Security

All operations happen locally on your device using temporary access tokens managed by iOS.
