About Us

Puls IT is a young and ambitious software studio that creates intuitive and meaningful mobile apps for iOS. Everything started with a simple idea — to build the kind of app we ourselves were missing.

We believe technology should simplify life, not complicate it. That’s why we focus on developing products that are both functional and delightful to use — combining clean design, reliability, and respect for user privacy.

At Puls IT, we constantly explore new technologies and approaches to turn bold ideas into reality. Every piece of feedback helps us grow and make our apps even better.

Join us on this journey — we’re just getting started.
